Background
My name is Bojian Wu (吴博剑). I am currently working as an Algorithm Engineer at City Brain Lab, Alibaba DAMO Academy. I received my Ph.D. degree from University of Chinese Academy of Sciences, advised by Prof. Hui Huang. Previously, I obtained the master's degree from University of Science and Technology of China under the supervision of Prof. Xuejin Chen. From July 2018, I spent half an year in The University of Tokyo, working with Prof. Yonghao Yue and Prof. Takeo Igarashi.
