ようこそ! My name is Bojian Wu(吴博剑). I am currently a Ph.D. candidate majoring in Computer Graphics in Shenzhen Institutes of Advanced Technology, and supervised by Prof. Hui Huang. Prior to starting my Ph.D. career, I obtained my master's degree from the University of Science and Technology of China under the supervision of Prof. Xuejin Chen.

From July 2018, I spent half an year in The University of Tokyo, working with Prof. Yonghao Yue and Prof. Takeo Igarashi, on a project about human computer interaction and machine learning.
